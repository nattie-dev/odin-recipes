Chicken Enchiladas with Cream of Chicken Soup

Here's step-by-step instructions on how to make it!

Prepare to make an easy, comforting dish that would will absolutely adore!

Who needs regular chicken when you can make a delicious enchilada dish that will leave you savoring it for days?

Here's the grocery list to get you started!

Chicken enchiladas ingredients

  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup

  • ½ cup sour cream

  • 1 tablespoon margarine

  • 1 onion, chopped

  • 1 teaspoon chili powder

  • 2 cups chopped cooked chicken breast

  • 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers, drained

  • 8 (8 inch) flour tortillas

  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

How to make chicken enchiladas with cream of chicken soup step-by-step

Here's a brief overview for what to expect in the chicken enchiladas making process.

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Mix soup and sour cream in a small bowl; set aside
  3. Melt margarine in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat.
  4. Add onion and chili powder, and sauté until tender.
  5. Stir in chicken, chile peppers, and 2 tablespoons of the soup mixture. Cook and stir until heated through
  6. Spread 1/2 cup of the soup mixture in a 9x13-inch baking dish
  7. Spoon about 1/4 cup of the chicken mixture down the center of each tortilla.
  8. up tortillas and place, seam-sides-down, in the baking dish.
  9. Spoon remaining soup mixture on top, and sprinkle with cheese.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and lightly browned, about 25 minutes.

And there you have it!

You now have some delicious chicken enchiladas with cream of chicken soup! Enjoy!
