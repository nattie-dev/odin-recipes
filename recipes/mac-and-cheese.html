Homemade Mac and Cheese

Here's step-by-step instructions on how to make it!

Who needs boxed stuff when you can make your own?

You'll have mac and cheese so good you wonder why u didn't have it homemade before!

Here's the grocery list to get you started!

Mac and Cheese ingredients

  • Macaroni
    • It all begins with a box of uncooked macaroni noodles :)

  • Butter and flour
    • This will be needed to make roux for the cheese sauce! As well as two tablespoons of butter to top it off.

  • Milk
    • Whole milk adds a nice rich flavor and texture

  • Cheese
    • For cheese we will use Chedder and Parmesan

  • Seasonings
    • Salt and pepper for the sauce, with paprika sprinkled over the topping

  • Bread crumbs
    • You can use store bought bread crumbs, or make your own at home!

How to make macaroni and cheese step-by-step

Here's a brief overview for what to expect in the mac and cheese making process.

  1. Boil the noodles, drain, and transfer to a prepared baking dish
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour it over the noodles, and stir
  3. Make the topping, spread it over mac and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika
  4. Bake the macaroni and cheese until the topping is golden brown

And there you have it!

You now have a lovely homemade mac and cheese! Enjoy!
